Twilio AI Skills are structured, curated knowledge files that teach AI coding agents (like Claude or Cursor) how to build with Twilio's 1,800+ endpoints following production-grade best practices. The skills use progressive disclosure architecture to keep context efficient and come in four types: Setup, Planner, Product, and Guardrail, helping developers avoid hallucinations and architectural mistakes while building communications systems faster.
